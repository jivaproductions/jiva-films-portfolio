Our Story

Jiva Films was founded with a singular vision: to capture love stories in their most authentic, elegant form. We blend photojournalism with fine art photography, creating images that feel both spontaneous and timeless.

Our team of experienced photographers travels worldwide, bringing a documentary approach to every wedding — finding the extraordinary within the ordinary, the quiet magic in each moment.
